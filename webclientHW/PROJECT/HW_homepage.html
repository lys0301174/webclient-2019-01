A SHORT STORY OF H&H

We are HELEN & HARD

founded in 1996 in Stavanger on Norway's west coast by Siv Helene Stangeland & Reinhard Kropf. Today, we are also located in Oslo.

We are ARCHITECTS

from 10 different countries, a youthful team on the move. Our collaborations and projects are increasingly international.

We DESIGN

in different scales & scopes, from single family houses to large public buildings, from offices and housing projects to master planning.

We are SUSTAINABLE

design, not only in the design of spaces, but also in the conception & organization of the design process, incuding building & fabrication.
